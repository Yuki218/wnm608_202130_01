Taco

1. In a medium-sized iron skillet,
cook the ground beef
and onion for about 8-10 minutes.

2. Fold in the sauce, chili powder,
salt, and garlic powder.
Mix well and reduce the heat to a simmer.

3. Bake the taco shells for about 5 minutes
until warm and slightly crisped.
Set aside to cool for about 5 minutes.

4. Assemble the tacos. Place the
ground beef mixture onto each taco.
Sprinkle over the cheese,
lettuce, and chopped tomatoes.
